## Featured Courses

## Introduction to Brain–Computer Interfacing (Fall 2016)

Explores foundational principles, major types, and applications of brain–computer interfaces integrating neuroscience and machine learning, with discussion on ethical considerations.

## Introduction to Neural and Cognitive Modeling (Spring 2016 & 2015)

Multi-scale computational approaches in neuroscience covering single-neuron, network, and abstract levels. Includes modeling of resting-state networks, hemodynamic processes, and connectionist frameworks.

## Research Methodology (Monsoon 2015)

A course on behavioral research design, experimental methods, data acquisition, and statistical interpretation for scientific reporting.

## Introduction to Cognitive Science (Spring 2015)

Examines neural mechanisms underlying sensory information processing—vision, audition, touch—and higher cognitive functions such as memory, language, and emotion.
